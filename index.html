Caracas - Venezuela

Caracas is the capital of Venezuela and the city where I grew up. It's surrounded by beautiful mountains, especially the Ávila, which is like a natural treasure for everyone who lives there. Caracas is full of life, with busy streets, warm people, and a mix of modern buildings and historical places. It’s a place that holds many memories for me, from its parks to its delicious food and vibrant culture.
